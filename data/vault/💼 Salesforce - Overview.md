## What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is the CRM platform used by the PAT team to manage all customer support cases. It's where you receive, review, assign, and close cases.

**Access link:** https://compucom.lightning.force.com/lightning

> 🚨 **NOTE:** Access to Salesforce must be granted by an administrator. In our case, Fernando Blengio requested our access via a support ticket.

> 🚨 **NOTE:** In order to access Salesforce, the user must have logged into MKP (Marketplace) first. To verify if a user has done this, search for them in Salesforce — if they don't appear in "Contacts", the user hasn't logged into MKP yet.

## Navigating to Commerce Storefront Console

From the Home view, navigate to the **Commerce Storefront Console**:

![[assets/doc-sf-app-launcher.png]]

1. Click the **App Launcher** button (9-dot grid icon)
2. Use the search bar to find **Apps and Items**
3. Select **Commerce Storefront Console**

The following view will be displayed:

![[assets/doc-sf-commerce-console.png]]

This view shows the content including different cases, their statuses, contacts, and other details.

---

## Accessing PAT Queue

To view the cases assigned to our Team Box:

1. Click the **downward arrow icon**
2. Select **Product Assistance Team (PAT) Queue**

![[assets/doc-sf-pat-queue-dropdown.png]]

The cases for our team box will then be displayed:

![[assets/doc-sf-pat-queue-cases.png]]

# 📋 PAT — Remove SKU from Price Rule

---

## What is This Process?

Removing a specific **SKU** from an existing price rule in the CPT. This is needed when a product should no longer be available to a customer at that price or through that rule.

---

## Step by Step

### STEP 1 — Go to Preview and Filter

Go to the **Preview** menu (this process can take a while, depending on how wide the catalog is for the customer). Filter the table by the SKU to remove, identifying which rule it belongs to.

![[assets/doc-cpt-preview-filter.png]]

---

### STEP 2 — Filter by Rule Number

When identified, exit the preview window and filter **Price Rules** by **Rule Number**. Then press **"Edit"**.

![[assets/doc-cpt-filter-rule-edit.png]]

---

### STEP 3 — Open Rule Values Editor

Press the **paper and pen icon** to edit the Price Rule values.

![[assets/doc-cpt-edit-icon.png]]

---

### STEP 4 — Select and Remove the SKU

In the displayed menu, select the SKU and press **Remove**.

![[assets/doc-cpt-remove-sku.png]]

---

### STEP 5 — Save and Close

Press **Save and Close**.

![[assets/doc-cpt-save-close.png]]

---

### STEP 6 — Generate Rules

Since the rule values have been changed, when generating the price rules, the selected option will be **No** (because it was modified, not newly created). Press **Generate** and then **No**.

![[assets/doc-cpt-generate-no.png]]

## Email Signature Configuration

![[assets/doc-sf-profile-menu.png]]

Click your profile icon; a menu will appear. Select **Settings** to access your account settings and update your email signature.

![[assets/doc-sf-email-nav.png]]

Once in **Settings**, use the left-hand menu to navigate to **Email → My Email Settings**. The email configuration panel will display, where you should verify that your first name and last name are correctly listed, along with your CompuCom email address.

### Signature Format

```
Full Name
Business System I
your.email@compucom.com
```

![[assets/doc-sf-email-settings.png]]

Select the **Salesforce Email Composer** option, and under **Subscriptions**, ensure that:
- ✅ **Email Alerts** is checked
- ✅ **Content Alerts as Daily Digest** is checked

Click **Save** to complete the configuration.

## Salesforce Navigation Shortcuts

![[assets/doc-sf-shortcuts.png]]

These shortcuts should be configured in this specific order for quick access:

1. **Home** — at the beginning of the list
2. **Cases** — ⚠️ most frequently used section
3. **Buyer Groups**
4. **CMS Workspaces**
5. **Accounts**
6. **Contacts**

> 💡 Maintaining this order ensures quick and efficient access to the features most commonly used during daily processes and case resolution activities.

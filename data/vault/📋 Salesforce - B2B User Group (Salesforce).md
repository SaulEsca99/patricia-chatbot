## What is This Process?

Assigning a user to a **Buyer Group** (B2B User Group) in Salesforce. This determines which product catalog and pricing rules the user has access to on the Marketplace.

---

## Step by Step

### STEP 1 — Search for the User Account

![[assets/doc-sf-b2b-search.png]]

In Salesforce, use the **search bar** to find the user account. Once the user is found, click on the user's name to open their account details.

---

### STEP 2 — Assign to User Group

![[assets/doc-sf-b2b-assign-group.png]]

The user's account details will be displayed. On the right-hand side, the **"B2B Assign to User Group"** section will appear:

1. In the search bar, enter the **name of the group** to which the user will be assigned
2. Select the group from the results
3. Click **"Next"**

✅ The new group has been successfully assigned to the user.

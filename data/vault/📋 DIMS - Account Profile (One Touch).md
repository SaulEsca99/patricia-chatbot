## What is This Process?

Creating a new **Account Profile** allows you to add a new customer to a group in DIMS. This uses the **One Touch Account Profile Form** available on the PAT SharePoint site.

---

## Step by Step

### STEP 1 — Access the PAT SharePoint Page

Open the following link: [Product Assistance Team - Home](https://compucomcorp.sharepoint.com/sites/ProductAssistanceTeam)

Once you've accessed the page, scroll down and look for the option titled **"One Touch Account Profile Form."**

![[assets/doc-account-profile-link.png]]

Click on **"One Touch Account Profile Form."**

---

### STEP 2 — Cancel the Default New Item View

Once you click the option, a view will appear displaying **"New Item."** At the top of this form, click on the **"Cancel"** option.

![[assets/doc-account-profile-cancel.png]]

---

### STEP 3 — Review the Workflow

After clicking "Cancel," a new view will be displayed where you can review the content of the **One Touch Account Profile workflow**.

![[assets/doc-account-profile-workflow.png]]

---

### STEP 4 — Locate and Open the Account Profile

Inside the workflow, locate the account profile to be created. Here you will add the new customer to a group. When opening it, a form with pre-filled fields will be displayed.

![[assets/doc-account-profile-form.png]]

---

### STEP 5 — View Account Profile Details in DIMS

To view the **Account Profile details**, you must open the following interface in DIMS: **"Click One PD."** This will allow the **Production Account Profile** to be displayed.

> 💡 **Click One PD** is accessed through the Network Drive `\\spw099nas02g\ClickOnePD`. See [[🔐 Access Requests]] for how to request this access.

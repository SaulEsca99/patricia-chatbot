## What is the Marketplace?

At CompuCom, one of the core business activities involves the **sale of equipment through its Marketplace platform**. The backend of this platform is supported by DIMS, where product details and related information are managed and reviewed.

**Marketplace link:** https://marketplace.compucom.com/

---

## Login

In this initial section, access the CompuCom Marketplace website. Upon entry, the following interface will be displayed:

![[assets/doc-mkt-email-entry.png]]

Inside the first red box, enter the email address provided by CompuCom (e.g.: `your.email@compucom.com`), and then click **Continue** or press **Enter**.

![[assets/doc-mkt-login.png]]

A modal window will appear where you must select your credentials to access the Marketplace.

---

## Home

Once you have successfully logged in, you will be redirected to the **Marketplace Home** view, where you can interact with the platform as a customer.

## Demo Access — ZZZ Corp

**ZZZ Corp** is a testing/demo environment that enables us to perform customer-facing actions in order to validate and test the system without affecting real data.

> 💡 Access to the ZZZ Corp demo must be requested. Fernando Blengio provided our team with the demo access.

## Marketplace–DIMS Relationship

**CompuCom Marketplace is integrated with DIMS.**

DIMS serves as the central platform for key order information, including:
- Order status
- Billing/collections
- Associated box group
- Related SKUs
- And more

To work with DIMS, the required application (sNetTerm) must be installed. See [[⚙️ DIMS - Installation]].
